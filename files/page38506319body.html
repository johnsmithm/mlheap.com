GPT Customer Support and Lead Generation
Lead generation demo
Client's Goal: Our client, a small business owner, wanted to improve their customer support and lead generation process by reducing the workload on their customer support team and increasing the number of leads generated. They were looking for an automated solution that could handle customer queries, provide quick responses, and collect lead information accurately.
My Contribution
As a Senior ML engineer, my contribution to the project was to develop a GPT-powered chatbot that could handle customer queries and lead generation tasks seamlessly. I worked on training the model with chatGPT, which allowed the chatbot to interact with customers in a natural language and provide quick solutions to their queries. Additionally, I integrated custom scripts to enable the chatbot to collect lead information such as name, email address, and phone number. The chatbot was also linked to CRM for lead tracking and management.
Project success
The GPT-powered chatbot was a major success, providing reliable customer support and generating leads seamlessly. It was able to handle 85% of customer queries without any human intervention, reducing the workload on the customer support team by a significant margin. The chatbot was able to collect lead information accurately, resulting in a 45% increase in leads generated. The CRM integration allowed the client to track and manage leads effectively, leading to a 25% increase in sales. With its ability to learn from customer interactions, the chatbot continued to improve over time, resulting in a better customer experience and more efficient lead generation process for the client.
